Yoga Postures

Dhanurasana, or Bow Pose : is a yoga pose where you lie on your stomach and lift your legs and chest while holding your ankles. It helps strengthen the back and improve flexibility

Ustrasana, or Camel Pose : is a yoga pose where you kneel and lean back to touch your heels. It helps stretch the front of your body and improves flexibility in the spine.

Vrksasana, or Tree Pose: is a balancing yoga pose where you stand on one leg and place the other foot on your inner thigh. This pose helps improve balance, focus, and strengthens the legs and core.

Setubandhasana, or Bridge Pose : is a yoga pose where you lie on your back, lift your hips, and press your feet and shoulders into the ground. It strengthens the back and legs while opening the chest and stretching the spine.
